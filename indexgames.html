Who says you can't learn and have fun at the same time?

Here you will find interactive wildlife related games for kids sure to lead to hours of fun and laughter. Helping children appreciate the great outdoors from the comfort of their own homes, opening up a new world of discovery and shaping the conservationists of the future.


Play the wildlife Maze Game!

Can you help the wildlife find their way back home?



How to play?

We need your help, some local wildlife have escaped, collect them and bring them home!
Using your mouse make your way make through the maze collecting as many wildlife as you can by clicking them.
Be careful not to hit any of the walls, or you will need to start again!
When messages pop up use the left and right arrow on the keyboard to select yes or no, and select your answser using the enter key.
Good luck on your wildlife adventure!

Test your Knowledge!

Quiz time! Find out how much you know about your local wildlife. Some facts might just surprise you!

How to play?

Read each question and click the correct answser.Then click the next question button. If at any point you want to chnage your answers click the previous question button. At the end of the quiz click to submit your answers and see what you got correct. Good luck!
